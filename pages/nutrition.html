Nutrition Plan Details

Performance Meal Plan

Example structure: breakfast, pre-workout snack, lunch, post-workout recovery meal, dinner, and bedtime protein.

  • Breakfast: Oats, banana, whey, nuts
  • Pre-workout: rice cakes + peanut butter
  • Post-workout: chicken, sweet potato, greens

Weight Management Plan

Focused on portion control, high-quality protein, and nutrient timing to support fat loss or lean mass gain.

  • Lean protein at every meal
  • Vegetable-forward plates with moderate carbs
  • Snacks: Greek yogurt, berries, mixed seeds

Recovery & Hydration

Carb + protein combos with electrolytes to support muscle repair and hydration after hard sessions.

  • Chocolate milk-style shake
  • Turkey avocado wrap
  • Hydration: water, coconut water, electrolyte blend

Plant-Powered Performance

Vegetarian meals designed for protein, micronutrients, and energy through whole foods.

  • Tofu stir-fry with quinoa
  • Lentil and sweet potato bowl
  • Nut butter + fruit smoothie

Pre-Competition Fuel

Low-fiber, high-carbohydrate meals leading into game day to top off energy stores without bloating.

  • Morning: pancakes + fruit
  • Evening before: pasta with lean protein
  • Day of: easy oatmeal, banana, water

Recovery Smoothie Plan

Fast, nutrient-dense shakes and snack ideas that are great after training or between sessions.

  • Berry protein smoothie
  • Green recovery smoothie
  • Peanut butter banana shake
